Modern

The "Modern" typographical theme represents a printed magazine with inspiration taken from magazines of around the latest century change. Magazines such as Vanity Fair, Vogue and TIME were referenced as examples of the typographical period, and as a consequence the final style resembles that of these magazines.

The color palette chosen is a simple one; black, white and red. This palette is common among all the referenced magazines, and an example from TIME magazine from 2000 is shown in the image below.
